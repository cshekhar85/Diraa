1. About Diraa Capital & smallcase
Q1: What is Diraa Capital?

Diraa Capital is a quantitative research and analytics firm delivering data-driven model portfolios. To provide a seamless execution experience, our institutional-grade strategies are packaged into smallcases—curated baskets of stocks or ETFs designed around specific market regimes and momentum factors.

Q2: What is a smallcase and how does it work?

A smallcase is a modern investment product that represents a basket of stocks or ETFs matching a precise strategy. Instead of manually researching, buying, and tracking individual stocks, you subscribe to a Diraa Capital smallcase, link your existing demat account, and invest in the entire portfolio with a single click.

Q3: Who can invest in Diraa Capital smallcases?

Our strategies are custom-built for disciplined, forward-thinking investors and active market participants looking for a systematic approach to wealth creation. Anyone with an active trading and demat account with an integrated Indian broker can invest.

Q4: Does Diraa Capital hold my capital or assets?

Absolutely not. Diraa Capital operates strictly as a research and advisory entity. Your funds remain entirely inside your personal brokerage account, and all purchased shares are held securely in your personal Demat account (CDSL/NSDL). We have zero custody over your money or shares.

Q5: Which brokers are supported?

Thanks to our partnership with smallcase, we support virtually all leading Indian stockbrokers. You can seamlessly log in and execute our strategies using your existing credentials from Dhan, Zerodha (Kite), Groww, Upstox, Angel One, ICICI Direct, HDFC Sky, and many others.

2. Investment Philosophy & Strategy
Q6: What is the underlying strategy behind your portfolios?

We do not rely on standard, stagnant buy-and-hold methodologies. Our models leverage advanced systematic screening, utilizing volume dynamics, structural trend-following, and market regime filters. We focus heavily on highly liquid, high-momentum equity segments to capture optimal upside while cutting lagging exposure.

Q7: How does Diraa Capital manage risk during market corrections?

Capital preservation is built directly into our quantitative filters. Every portfolio features built-in risk controls, including liquidity guardrails, diversification caps, and algorithmic regime switches. When market environments turn choppy or unfavorable, our models systematically reduce portfolio beta and scale back exposure to preserve your capital.

Q8: Can I manually customize a Diraa Capital smallcase?

While the smallcase interface allows you to manually add, remove, or alter stock weights, we strongly advise against it. Our portfolios run on precise systematic algorithms and risk filters. Modifying the constituents alters the core strategy logic, which means your real-world performance will diverge from Diraa Capital’s verified returns.

3. Execution & Operations
Q9: How does the portfolio rebalancing process work?

As market conditions evolve, we dynamically adjust the constituents or weights of the smallcase. When a rebalance occurs, you will receive an instant notification via the smallcase app, email, or WhatsApp. To apply the updates, you simply log into your dashboard, review the changes, and click "Apply Rebalance" to update your entire portfolio in seconds.

Q10: Can I set up a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)?

Yes. You can set up an automated SIP on a weekly, fortnightly, or monthly basis directly through the smallcase platform. The system automatically calculates the required amount based on live market prices and prompts you to execute the orders with a single click on your scheduled date.

Q11: What happens to the dividends paid by companies in my smallcase?

Since you directly own the underlying shares in your personal Demat account, all corporate benefits belong entirely to you. Any dividends declared by the companies are credited directly to your linked bank account, while benefits like stock splits or bonuses apply automatically to your Demat holdings.

4. Fees, Account Management & Taxes
Q12: What is the minimum investment requirement?

The minimum investment is not fixed; it is calculated dynamically based on the current market prices and exact weightings of the individual stocks within that specific basket. This ensures that the structural integrity and risk allocation of the strategy are perfectly maintained.

Q13: How does Diraa Capital’s fee structure work?

We believe in complete transparency. Diraa Capital operates on a flat-fee subscription model (processed securely through smallcase) rather than charging a percentage of your total assets under management (AUM). There are no hidden performance cuts or variable management charges, ensuring you keep 100% of your compounding returns.

Q14: How are taxes handled during a rebalance?

When a rebalance instruction requires selling a stock or reducing its allocation, it is treated as a standard sell transaction on your broker account. Depending on how long you held the asset, standard Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) or Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax rules apply. You can download a clean, consolidated capital gains tax report directly from your smallcase profile at the end of the financial year.

Q15: What happens if I choose to cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you retain 100% ownership of all the stocks currently sitting in your Demat account. Nothing is automatically liquidated. However, you will immediately stop receiving our dynamic rebalancing updates, market regime alerts, and strategy shifts, leaving your portfolio static from that point forward.
